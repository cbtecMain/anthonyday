Sub Perks

Tier 1 - £3.99 Monthly

Tier 1 is the cheapest subscription plan at £3.99 monthly.
A Tier 1 subscription will award you with 5 animated emotes and 9 regular emotes
in Twitch chat. Any subscription will award you with the ability to join games.
In Battle Royale and shooters, such as Call Of Duty and Fortnite, you are allocated
2 games unless there is a redo or a win (only to be allocated once). Tier 1 allows
you into the higher odds, tier 1 giveaway that occurs at the end of the calendar month.
At subscription date, you will be awarded 4 StreamLoots crates as a
Tier 1 base.
All subscriptions allow advertisement free viewing.

Tier 2 - £7.99 Monthly

Tier 2 is a slightly more premium subscription plan at £7.99 monthly.
A Tier 2 subscription will award you with 5 animated emotes and 10 regular emotes
in Twitch chat. Any subscription will award you with the ability to join games.
In Battle Royale and shooters, such as Call Of Duty and Fortnite, you are allocated
4 games unless there is a redo or a win (only to be allocated once). Tier 2 allows
you into the lower odds, Tier 2 and 3 giveaway that occurs at the end of the calendar month.
This gives you a better chance on winning a seperate but equally valued giveaway.
At subscription date, you will be awarded 8 StreamLoots crates as a
Tier 2 bonus.
All subscriptions allow advertisement free viewing.

Tier 3 - £19.99 Monthly

Tier 3 is the premium and most expensive subscription plan at £19.99 monthly.
A Tier 3 subscription will award you with 5 animated emotes and 11 regular emotes
in Twitch chat. Any subscription will award you with the ability to join games.
In Battle Royale and shooters, such as Call Of Duty and Fortnite, you are allocated
Unlimited games unless there is a redo or a win (only to be allocated once). Tier 3 allows
you into the lower odds, Tier 2 and 3 giveaway that occurs at the end of the calendar month.
This gives you a better chance on winning a seperate but equally valued giveaway.
At subscription date, you will be awarded 12 StreamLoots crates as a
Tier 3 bonus.
All subscriptions allow advertisement free viewing.
